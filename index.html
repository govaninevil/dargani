Our Journey

Dargani Steel Pvt. Ltd. (An ISO 9001 Company) was established in the year 2003 in Gujarat, India. Dargani Steel has invested in high-end equipment that includes CNC machines, press brakes, punch presses and saws. This allows us to produce products with accuracy and precision. We have also invested in state-of-the-art CAD/CAM systems to ensure that the products like Stainless Steel Washer, Furniture Items, Coil and Sheet we offer meet our customers’ specifications. We also have a fully equipped quality control lab to ensure that all of our products meet the highest standards of quality.


Our team of experienced professionals work together to ensure that all of our products are manufactured to the highest standards and that our customer’s requirements are met. We strive to provide our customers with the highest quality products and services that meet their requirements and exceed their expectations. We are committed to providing our customers with the best value for their money and we take great pride in our ability to meet the needs of our customers.
